Mercedes-AMG GT R

Overview

The Mercedes AMG GT-R is the highest end of the AMG GT line up. This is a track focused car with amazing performance and handling. This is also in my opinion one of the most beautiful cars ever made

Key Features

  • 4.0L Hand-Built Twin-Turbo V8 Engine
  • 577 Horsepower and 516 lb-ft of Torque
  • Active Aerodynamics and Rear-Wheel Steering
  • Lightweight aluminum and carbon fiber construction
  • Track-tuned suspension and performance electronics
