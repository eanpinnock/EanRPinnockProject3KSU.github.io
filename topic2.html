Mercedes-AMG GT R

Overview

The Mercedes-AMG GT R is a track-focused car with outstanding performance and handling.

History

The Mercedes AMG GT-R was initially introduced in 2014 at the Paris Motor Show. It was developed by Mercedes-Benz performance division, AMG(Aufrecht, Melcher and Großaspach,). This vehicle was the second sports car to be developed entirely in house by Mercedes-AMG. The AMG GT is the successor to the previous SLS AMG. The highest trim level for the Mercedes AMG GT-R is the black series.

Key Features

  • 4.0L Hand-Built Twin-Turbo V8 Engine
  • 577 Horsepower
  • Active Aerodynamics and Rear-Wheel Steering
  • Lightweight construction with aluminum and carbon fiber
