Key Concepts

Powertrain:

The components that generate and deliver power to the road.

Horsepower:

Unit of power output from the engine.

Features:

Equipment and technology on the car.

Performance:

How the car behaves dynamically (acceleration, handling).

Turbocharger:

A device to force more air into the engine for increased power.
