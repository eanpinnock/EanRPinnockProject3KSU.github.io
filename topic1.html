Nissan GT-R Nismo

Overview

The Nissan GT-R Nismo is a high-performance variant of the legendary GT-R, known for its incredible acceleration and handling.

History

The Nissan GT-R is the successor to the Nissan Skyline GT-R introduced in 1969. This car went through many different iterations before Nissan reached the modern GT-R, or the R35. It was unveiled in 2007. The highest trim level for the Nissan GT-R is the Nismo package. Despite the relatively low price when compared to similar supercars, the GT-R has comparable performance.

Key Features

  • 3.8L Twin-Turbo V6
  • 600 Horsepower
  • Advanced All-Wheel Drive
  • Carbon fiber body components
